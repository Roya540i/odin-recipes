EASY Penne Pesto Chicken

Description

This is a simple and delicious recipe for Penne Pesto Chicken that's ideal for meal prep!

The quanities in this recipe will make 4 servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 2 cups of asparagus, cut into 1.5in pieces
  • 10oz of cherry tomatoes
  • 2 cups chickpea penne pasta
  • 2/3 cup pesto

Steps

  1. Begin boiling a pot of water for the pasta
  2. While waiting for the water to boil, begin cooking the chicken. Ensure a good sear.
  3. Cook the pasta and finish the chicken. Dice the chicken into cubes.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet. Toss in the asparagus and cook for about 3 minutes to soften.
  5. Add in the pesto, pasta, and the chicken then stir to combine.
  6. Toss in the cherry tomatoes and stir again.
  7. After a few minutes, remove everything and distribute into separate containers for a week of meal prep completed!
